// Process

How It Works

01
Browse & Choose
Pick one or more models from the catalog. Can't find your shape? Request a custom model built to your specs.
02
Contact & Pay
Reach out on Facebook. Payment via PayPal in USD. Fast response, no middlemen.
03
Receive the File
Get your Fusion 360 native file (.f3d) delivered directly. Open it, review it, and set up your own toolpath for your machine and tools.
04
Cut & Build
Generate your CAM toolpath in Fusion 360 or export to your preferred CAM software. Cut the body on your CNC and start building.

// File Formats

Delivered Formats

.F3D — Fusion 360 Native
.STEP — Universal CAD
.STL — On Request
.DXF — On Request

Files are delivered as 3D solid models. The buyer is responsible for setting up their own toolpath based on their CNC machine, tooling, and workflow.
Selected models include the Fusion 360 CAM setup — these are marked CAM READY in the catalog.
